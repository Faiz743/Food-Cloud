About Us

Food Cloud is an online food order website where customer can easily order food through online according to there interest. We are committed to nurturing a neutral platform and are helping food establishments maintain high standards through Hyperpure. We not only connect people to food in every context but work closely with restaurants to enable a sustainable ecosystem. We push ourselves beyond our abilities when faced with tough times.Feedback is never taken personally, we break it into positive pieces and strive to work on each and every element even more effectively. We don’t lose ourselves in pride or confidence during individual successes, but focus on being our simple selves in every which way. We believe in, stand for and are evangelists of our culture - both, within FoodCloud and externally with all our stakeholders. When your goal is to change the way the country eats, you need all the help you can get. The business of food delivery is undergoing rapid change every day as we are capturing markets and customers across the country. We aim to get these right, at least in the majority of the cases.
